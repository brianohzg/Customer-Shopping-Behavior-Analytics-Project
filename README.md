## 📈 Results & Recommendations
- **Key Insights:**
  - High‑value customers shop during weekends and prefer bundled discounts  
  - Certain product categories drive repeat purchases  
  - Younger demographics respond strongly to seasonal promotions  

- **Business Recommendations:**
  - Focus marketing on weekend campaigns  
  - Expand bundled offers to increase basket size  
  - Target seasonal promotions to younger segments
